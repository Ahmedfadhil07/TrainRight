Why I Built This

I've gone through upgrade training three times — 3-level, 5-level, and 7-level — and every single time, the hardest part wasn't learning the material. It was finding the material. Outdated CDCs, scattered study guides in random group chats, trainers who were TDY when I needed them most. I built this site so the next Airman doesn't have to waste time hunting for answers I already have.

My Journey

Year 1 - Basic Training & Tech School

Started my career field, learned the fundamentals, and quickly realized how disorganized the study materials were.

Year 2 - 3-Level to 5-Level Upgrade

Struggled to find consistent OJT tracking. Started keeping my own personal notes and checklists.

Year 4 - 5-Level to 7-Level Upgrade

Became the shop's unofficial "go-to" person for upgrade questions. Realized this needed to scale beyond just me.

Year 6 - Certified 7-Level Trainer

Built this site to formalize what I'd been doing informally for years — helping Airmen upgrade faster and with less stress.

My Training Philosophy

Speed and clarity matter. If it takes you 20 minutes to find a study guide, that's 20 minutes you're not actually learning. Everything on this site is organized so you can find what you need in seconds, not minutes.

— Jordan
